About Me

My name is Kvothe. I consider myself sharp-tounged and wit, but I also consider myself as a fool in rare occasions. I have pale skin and green eyes , however people say that my eyes change color according my mood and atmosphere. I have extremeley red hair often compared to a flame.

I am of the Edma-Ruh, which means I travel a lot, never settling at one location. I love to perform and play the lute. I could not get enough of the lute, it is part of me and as a musician would know, is also the most important object i own. The first lute I've owned was broken by a bully like kid in Tarbean. Later I have acquired a new lute during my time at the University. However it was also broken by a man named Ambrose, which then bought me a new lute after a trial at the University, because I hurt him once he broke it. How else a musician would react with nothing in the world beside his instrument broken.
